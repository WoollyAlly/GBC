Homebirth Class

For all first time parents and those who have only labored in a hospital previously, GBC asks that you join us for this fun, informative class during your 3rd trimester!

Homebirth is incredible! We are so happy you have chosen this path for your birth. There are quite a few details, supplies, and general tidbits of information that can be incredibly helpful
while preparing for and navigation your first homebirth. We will discuss stages of labor, set-up for your home, details about transfers and interventions, postpartum guidelines, and more!
Come join us to learn, share, and meet other parents along your journey.

Occurs: Every 4th Tuesday of the month from 7:00pm-9:00pm
